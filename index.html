Conway's Game of Life

Conway's Game of Life involves cells that live, die, or become reborn through a few different scenarios.

These scenarios are:
  • A Cell dies if it has 0 or 1 neighbor, it starves
  • A Cell dies if it has 4 or more neighbors, it's over-populated
  • A Cell is reborn if it has exactly 3 living neighbors, it's magic
While the game is not running, feel free to edit the cells by clicking them.
